Frequently Asked Questions

What is the student-to-teacher ratio? expand_more
Our average class size is 18 students, ensuring personalized attention. In A-Level classes, the ratio is typically 8:1 for specialized subjects.
Do you offer scholarships or financial aid? expand_more
Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for academic, sports, and arts excellence. Applications for scholarships must be submitted by January 31st for the following academic year.
Can international students apply? expand_more
Absolutely. St. Scholar Academy welcomes students from over 30 countries. We assist with student visa applications and offer comprehensive ELL support for non-native English speakers.
