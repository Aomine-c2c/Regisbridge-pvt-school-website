How to Process Termly Results

Sarah Jenkins
Updated 2 days ago
schedule 5 min read

Processing termly results is a critical end-of-term activity. This guide walks you through entering raw scores, verifying grade distributions, and submitting the final report card data for administrative approval. Ensure accuracy to prevent errors in student transcripts.

1 Prerequisites

Checklist before starting

  • check_circle Ensure all class attendance registers for the term are closed.
  • check_circle Verify that the Grading Scale (A-F or 1-100) is correctly configured in Settings.
  • radio_button_unchecked Confirm you have "Teacher" or "Registrar" access permissions.

2 Step 1: Entering Raw Scores

Navigate to Academics > Grading > Marksheet. Select your class and subject from the dropdown filters. The grid view allows for rapid data entry similar to a spreadsheet.

Click to expand
lightbulb

Pro Tip: Bulk Fill

Use the "Bulk Fill" action in the top right menu for elective subjects where most students receive the same participation grade.

3 Step 2: Interactive Simulation

Before working on live data, familiarize yourself with the interface using this interactive simulator. Try entering scores greater than 100 to see the validation error, or notice how the grade auto-calculates.

4 Step 3: Approval & Finalization

Once all grades are entered, the system will calculate the GPA automatically. Click the "Submit for Approval" button at the bottom of the marksheet. This will lock the records and send a notification to the Head of Department.

  • You cannot edit grades after submission.
  • If a correction is needed, request an "Unlock" from the admin dashboard.
