How to Find Us

directions_car Driving Directions

From City Center (N1 Highway)

Take Exit 24 towards Oak Avenue. Turn left at the first traffic light onto St. Marks Drive. Continue straight for 2km. The main gate will be on your right.

From International Airport

Follow signs for R21 South. Merge onto the N1 North. Take the Elm Street off-ramp and turn right. Follow the signs for "St. Marks Academy".
