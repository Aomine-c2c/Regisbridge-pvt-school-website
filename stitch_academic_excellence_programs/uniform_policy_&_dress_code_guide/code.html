Uniform Policy & Dress Code

Our uniform is a symbol of our community's pride and heritage. This guide outlines the required standards of appearance for all students, fostering a sense of belonging and discipline.

High School Uniform Requirements

*All items must be clearly labeled with student name.
Number 1s

Formal Dress

Required for Monday assembly, chapel services, and formal school events.

  • check_circle Blazer: Maroon with embroidered school crest on pocket.
  • check_circle Tie: Senior house tie or silver prefect tie.
  • check_circle Shirt: Crisp white long-sleeve, top button done up.
Terms 1 & 4

Summer Day Wear

Daily wear for warmer months. Blazer optional outside of formal events.

  • check_circle Shirt: Short-sleeve white open-neck shirt with crest.
  • check_circle Bottoms: Grey trousers or knee-length charcoal skirt.
  • check_circle Socks: Grey ankle socks only.
Physical Ed

Sports Kit

Standard kit for PE and team practices. Match kit varies by sport.

  • check_circle Top: House color polo or team jersey.
  • check_circle Tracksuit: Official maroon school tracksuit for winter.
Terms 2 & 3

Winter Additions

Supplementary items for colder weather.

  • check_circle Pullover: Grey v-neck pullover with maroon trim.
  • check_circle Scarf: Official school colors only.
policy

Dress Code Standards

face

Hair & Grooming

Hair must be neat, clean, and of a natural color. For boys, hair must be off the collar and ears. For girls, long hair must be tied back with maroon, black, or white ribbons/bands.

watch

Jewelry & Accessories

Girls: One pair of plain silver or gold studs in earlobes only.
All: A simple analog or digital watch is permitted. No smartwatches during exams.

do_not_step

Footwear

Black polished leather lace-up shoes are required with formal uniform. No suede, buckles, or decorative stitching. Sports shoes must be predominantly white.
