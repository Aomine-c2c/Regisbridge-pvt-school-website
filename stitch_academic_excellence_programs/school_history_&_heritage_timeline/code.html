Our Journey

"We are not merely makers of history. We are made by history."

From our humble beginnings in a single schoolhouse to a sprawling campus of innovation, walk through the moments that defined St. Mary's Academy.

1920

The Foundation

St. Mary's Academy was founded by Rev. Thomas Miller. The cornerstone was laid on a brisk autumn morning, marking the beginning of a legacy devoted to academic rigor and moral fortitude.

Archive No. 001
Archive No. 042
1955

Boarding House Opens

The expansion era brought students from across the country. The opening of Miller Hall provided a home away from home, fostering a tight-knit community spirit that remains today.

1984

Science Wing Inauguration

Embracing modernization, the Academy unveiled state-of-the-art laboratories. This marked a pivotal shift towards STEM excellence, producing generations of doctors and engineers.

2002

The Digital Age

St. Mary's was among the first in the region to integrate a fully connected digital campus, launching the Computer Science department and the new Media Resource Center.

Today

A Global Campus

Today, St. Mary's Academy stands as a beacon of holistic education, with diverse students from over 30 countries. We continue to honor our traditions while preparing leaders for tomorrow's challenges.
