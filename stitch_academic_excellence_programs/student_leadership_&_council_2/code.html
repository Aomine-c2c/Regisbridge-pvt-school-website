What is the application deadline for the 2025 academic year? expand_more

Applications for the 2025 academic year close on September 30th, 2024. However, we encourage early applications as spaces in certain grades fill up quickly. Late applications are considered on a case-by-case basis subject to availability.

View Admission Timeline arrow_forward
Do you offer scholarships or financial aid? expand_more

Yes, Prestige Academy offers a limited number of academic, sporting, and cultural scholarships for Grade 8 entry. Financial aid bursaries are also available for families demonstrating significant financial need. Applications for financial assistance must be submitted alongside the main admission application.

What documents are required for the entrance assessment? expand_more

On the day of the assessment, please ensure your child brings their latest school report card, a copy of their birth certificate, and a passport-sized photograph. Stationary will be provided by the school. The assessment covers Mathematics, English, and a Non-Verbal Reasoning test.

Is there a sibling discount for tuition fees? expand_more

We offer a 5% discount on tuition fees for the second sibling and a 10% discount for the third and subsequent siblings enrolled simultaneously at Prestige Academy. This discount applies to tuition fees only and excludes boarding or levy fees.

Can prospective parents tour the boarding facilities? expand_more

Absolutely. We believe seeing our boarding houses is essential for peace of mind. Tours are conducted every Wednesday afternoon and on designated Open Days. Please contact the Admissions Office to book a slot, as for security reasons, walk-ins cannot be accommodated in the boarding houses.
